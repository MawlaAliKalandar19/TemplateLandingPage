Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CurvLife Nail Glue, answered for you.

How long does the glue last on nails?

With proper application, CurvLife Nail Glue can keep your press-on nails securely in place for up to 3 weeks. Many users report their nails lasting through daily activities like hand washing, typing, and even light gardening without any issues.

How do I apply the glue correctly?

For best results: 1) Prep your nails by gently filing the surface and cleaning with alcohol, 2) Apply a thin layer of glue to your natural nail, 3) Press the artificial nail firmly for 15-20 seconds until secure, 4) Avoid water contact for at least 30 minutes after application.

Is the remover effective?

Yes, the included nail polish remover features a dropper design that effectively penetrates tight spaces between nails. It typically removes glued nails in just 2-3 minutes without the need for laborious scraping or prying.

Can beginners use this product?

Absolutely! CurvLife Nail Glue is perfect for beginners. The semi-solid formula is drip-proof and easy to control, and the quick-drying feature means you don't need to hold nails in place for long periods. Many first-time users achieve professional-looking results.

Is the glue safe for natural nails?

When used as directed and removed properly with the included remover, CurvLife Nail Glue is safe for natural nails. The formula is designed to provide strong adhesion without causing damage to the natural nail bed when removed correctly.
